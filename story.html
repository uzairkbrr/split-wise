Whenever we went out for chai or food, everyone would look at me like, "Uzair bhai, pay kar do, baad mein le lena" (Pay now, take later). My wallet was basically public property.

Whether it was Late night chai at Food street or just Biryani at Abid Bhai's Dhabba, I was always the one fronting the cash. "Don't worry, we'll Splitwise it," they said. But we never did.

Trying to chase them down for 50 PKR was a mission impossible. I was losing money faster than I was losing my sanity. I needed a way to track this chaos without needing a degree in advanced accounting.
