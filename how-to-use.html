How to Use

Master your expense management in 3 simple steps.

1 Add People

Start by adding the people involved. Click the Add Person button on the dashboard. Enter their name and they will be added to your tracking list initially with a balance of 0.00.

2 Add Expenses

Click the Add Expense button. Enter the total amount, how many people are splitting it, who paid, and select the names of the people involved. The app will automatically calculate who owes what.

3 Settle Up Benefits

When someone pays you back (or you pay them), click the Settle Up button. Select who is paying whom and the amount. This records a "Settlement" transaction and adjusts the balances accordingly, helping you reach zero!

4 Export Data

Want a hard copy of your finances? Click the Export Data button to generate a Detailed PDF Report containing visual graphs, math explanations, and a complete transaction history.

How does it calculate everything? It uses a simple "Split Equitably" algorithm:

  • Total Amount / N: Every expense is divided by the number of people (N) involved.
  • Payer Credit: The person who paid gets a POSITIVE credit for the full amount.
  • Consumer Debit: Everyone involved (including the payer!) gets a NEGATIVE debit for their share.

Example: You pay 300 for 3 people (A, B, C).
You get +300. Then A, B, and C each get -100.
Your Net: +200 (Because you paid 300 but consumed 100).
Others Net: -100 each (They owe you).
